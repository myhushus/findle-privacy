1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how HBN ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects the personal data of users in the European Union (EU) and European Economic Area (EEA) in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data Controller

The data controller for your personal information is HBN, based in Korea. For any inquiries, you can contact us at director@hbn.co.kr.

3. Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data from users:

  • Name

  • Hashed password
  • Photos uploaded by users, including but not limited to profile pictures
  • Usage data (e.g., interactions within the app)
  • Device information (e.g., OS, model, IP address)

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following legal bases as defined by the GDPR:

  • Consent (e.g., when you sign up or upload a profile photo)
  • Contractual necessity (e.g., providing you with our services)
  • Legitimate interest (e.g., app analytics and improvements)
  • Legal obligation (e.g., to comply with applicable laws)

5. Purposes of Processing

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Improve and personalize the service
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud and misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

6. International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the EU/EEA, such as Korea. We ensure such transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

8. Your GDPR Rights

As a user in the EU/EEA, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of your personal data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We will notify you of any material changes in advance where required.

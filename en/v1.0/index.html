Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 01, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Findle ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored as a hashed value only)
  • Profile photo

2. How We Collect Information

  • Information you provide during account registration
  • Google account details if you sign up via Google login

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To enable user search and matching features
  • To respond to inquiries and support requests
  • To process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • To prevent unauthorized use and protect user security

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary for service provision or as required by law. Upon account deletion, personal data is permanently erased unless retention is legally mandated.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your data. In certain cases, we may share your information with trusted third-party services (e.g., payment processors) only for the purposes outlined above.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. For any requests, please contact us at director@hbn.co.kr.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies in our app.

8. International Users

Your information may be stored and processed in countries outside of your residence. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your data is protected.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable laws. Parental consent is required for users under 16 in certain regions.

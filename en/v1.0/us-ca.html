Privacy Policy (California Version)

Effective date: July 01, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how HBN ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects the personal information of California residents in accordance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

2. Data Controller

The data controller for your personal information is HBN, based in Korea. For any inquiries, you can contact us at director@findle.me.

3. Categories of Personal Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Identifiers (e.g., name, email address)
  • Photos (e.g., profile pictures)
  • Internet or other electronic network activity (e.g., usage data)
  • Device information (e.g., OS, IP address)
  • In-app messages or content (e.g., chat messages)

4. Use of Personal Information

We use the collected personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To personalize user experience
  • To communicate with users
  • To improve the app
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Sharing of Personal Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share personal data with service providers solely for operational purposes, under agreements that comply with CCPA/CPRA.

6. Your California Privacy Rights

As a California resident, you have the following rights under the CCPA/CPRA:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • The right to request access to your personal data
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal data
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal information
  • The right to limit the use of sensitive personal information
  • The right not to be discriminated against for exercising any of your rights

7. Children's Privacy

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), Findle does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. For users between the ages of 13 and 16, we do not sell or share personal data without affirmative authorization. If we discover that personal information has been collected from a California resident under these age limits without the appropriate consent, we will delete it immediately. To exercise your privacy rights or report a concern, please contact director@findle.me.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. If we make material changes, we will notify you as required by law.
